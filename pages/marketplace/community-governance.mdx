# NFT-Based Voting 

Introduce a governance NFT specific to Poply NFT marketplace.

 NFT holders can participate in voting on various proposals and decisions. The number of NFT held by each voter determines their voting power. This ensures that the decision-making process reflects the opinions of token holders.

 ### Proposals and Voting Mechanism: 
 Establish a process for submitting and evaluating proposals. This can involve a formal submission system where community members can propose changes or ( List , Delist Collections) , improvements, or initiatives. The community then votes on these proposals through a secure and transparent mechanism.
